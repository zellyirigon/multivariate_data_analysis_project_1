
Project 1: Process of Multivariate Exploratory Analysis for the Marketing Area

In this project, the goal is to conduct an extensive data exploration and identify factors that help explain why a 
customer accepts or rejects a marketing campaign and purchases certain products. Analysis will be carried out and 
provide a conclusion for decision-makers. Throughout the process, various multivariate analysis techniques will be
applied.
